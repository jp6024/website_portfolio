CHARACTERS

KAZ BREKKER

● A thief with a gift for unlikely escapes.


Also known as "Dirtyhands", Kaz Brekker is effectively the leader of the Dregs, one of the gangs in the Barrel, in Ketterdam, Kerch.


He has a fearsome reputation of doing anything and everything to get what he wants, and he doesn't care who he has to knock down on the way.


INEJ GHAFA

● A spy known as the Wraith.


Known as the "Wraith", Inej is a spy for the Dregs. She is a Suli girl whose parents trained her in acrobatics, and is able to scale and break in and out of buildings.


She was once under contract with the Menagerie after she was captured by slavers. Now, she is searching for her parents.


JESPER FAHEY

● A sharpshooter who can't walk away from a wager.


The 'sharpshooter' of the group, Jesper is a talented marksman. He moved to Kerch from Noyvi Zem, where he once worked with his father on a farm.


Jesper left in order to attend university, but became addicted to gambling, and later joined the Dregs as a result. He is friendly, witty, and sarcastic.


WYLAN VAN ECK

● A runaway with a priviledged past.


The son of Jan Van Eck. He joins the team in rescuing Bo Yul-Bayur from the Ice Court because of his knowledge in demolitions and his use as a hostage in negotiating the reward.


Like his mother, Wylan is skilled in various arts, gravitating towards painting and music.


NINA ZENIK

● A Heartrender using her magic to survive the slums.


A Grisha and part of the Corporalki group who trained at the Little Palace in order to become a Heartrender. She worked with Zoya Nazyalensky, another powerful Grisha.


After she was captured by Matthias and escaped by lying, she found herself contracted to work at the House of the White Rose.

MATTHIAS HELVAR

● A convict with a thirst for revenge


A Fjerdan soldier — also known as a drüskelle. He was put into Hellgate after being falsely accused by Nina of being a slave trader. Years in confinement have made him cold and brutal, hateful and unforgiving.


After being freed by the Dregs, he ends up becoming a pivotal member on their journey to complete the heist.

KUWEI YUL-BO

● And a boy with the key to it all.


The son of Bo Yul-Bayur, the scientist who manufactured the deadly jurda parem drug. Because of Kuwei's knowledge on the drug, many factions are hunting him.


Currently taking residence in the Ice Court. He does not approve of how their research on jurda was used.
